Typography 1

course
description

learning
outcomes

  • Recognize basics of typographic anatomy, classification, and terminology.
  • Execute clear typographic hierarchy and basic grid structures in visual communication problems.
  • Demonstrate a clear understanding of how design principles (such as compositional order, tension, scale, contrast, alignment, figure / ground) apply to typography.
  • Distinguish between well designed and poorly crafted typefaces.
  • Develop proficiency in digital typesetting (InDesign).
