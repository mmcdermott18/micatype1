p2: translations

Words on their own carry a lot of meaning. That meaning can be emphasized by how the type is treated. In a very simple example, look at the difference between "bold" and "BOLD". The word didn't change but making the word all caps and bold made the word more impactful. You will get a list of words to use and express through typographic transformations.

Step 1

Pick ten of the words in the list below and set them in Futura Bold. When setting them take into account the meaning of the word and try to express the meaning of the word by what it is doing on the page.

  • Transition
  • Expansion
  • Migration
  • Elimination
  • Repetition
  • Compression
  • Disruption
  • Silence
  • Softness
  • Loudness
  • Heavy
  • Lightness
  • Addition
  • Subtraction
  • Movement
