p1: paper letters

You will work on making letters from black and white paper focusing on expressive and unique letters, not replicating letters that already exist. You will start by making one letter and then use that letter to create more letters making sure the letters belong next to each other. You will then digitize the letters and further refine them. The goal of this project is to begin to closely examine letterforms and start to notice different aspects of typefaces.

Step 1

On a white sheet of 18x24 paper make a grid of 6 columns and 4 rows. The paper should be landscape. Cut out a black shape and glue it in one of the squares. Then cut out two white shapes to create a lowercase e and glue them on top of the black shape. Repeate this process until you have 24 e's on you pager.

Step 2

Pick an e from your 24 e's and using the same process as before create your name. If you name is too long you can use a shorter version but we are looking for at least 4 or so letters.
